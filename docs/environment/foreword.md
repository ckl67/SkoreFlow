# Foreword

From upload to structured music data — cleanly processed, SkoreFlow is based on an original idea proposed on [SheetAble](hhttps://github.com/ScoreAble/SheetAble).
However SkoreFlow is completely different, and has been designed with a full new modern REST architecture with much more features, and possibility of cool enhancements.

It is also open sourced, and free.
